## What is Melone?

Melone is a local-first memory engine for your desktop.

It observes your computer activity, organizes it into a searchable memory graph, and makes that context available to AI agents. Instead of starting every interaction from a blank prompt, your agents can understand what you were looking at, what you were working on, and how your work unfolded over time.

## What Melone helps with

- Search your recent desktop activity
- Recover what you were working on
- Give AI agents context from your actual workflow
- Build local memory across apps, windows, and sessions
- Support agents that understand your intent, taste, and working style
